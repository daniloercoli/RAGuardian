Why companies choose RAGuardian

There are hundreds of AI chatbots on the market. Most are SaaS that send your data to third-party servers or force everyone into one shared knowledge base. RAGuardian is self-hosted, multi-provider and multi-user: central control, personal RAG for every account.

Key advantages

8 reasons to choose RAGuardian

  1. Zero vendor lock-in Switch between Regolo.ai, Mistral AI and local models without touching a line of code. Your data investment is protected, regardless of the provider.
  2. Compliance by design With Regolo.ai, European inference, zero retention. No data transfer outside the EU. With a local model, data never leaves your network.
  3. Air-gapped mode Use a local model and no data ever leaves your datacenter. For highly regulated sectors, this option doesn't exist on any SaaS product.
  4. Open-source, auditable MIT License. Your security team can audit every line of code. No "black box" — you know exactly where your data goes.
  5. Personal workspaces, not separate installs One deployment can serve a whole office: each user has isolated files, email, Drive, conversations and Chroma collection. Admins manage users and providers from one place.
  6. Better candidates before reranking Candidate Diversity can stay off, cap chunks per source, or use MMR to balance Chroma relevance with semantic variety before the reranker sees the pool.
  7. Integration-ready WordPress plugin included, versioned REST API, workspace API keys with scopes, NDJSON streaming. We integrate into your stack, not the other way around.
  8. Personal connectors IMAP email and Microsoft Drive automatically populate the owning user's RAG. Encrypted credentials, workspace snapshots, async jobs and source metadata tracking.
